About Tiger Crab Cebu

Fresh Seafood, Filipino Flavor, Great Company

Tiger Crab Cebu serves fresh seafood feasts in Mandaue City for families, barkadas, tourists, and seafood lovers. Our dishes are made for sharing, from crab specialties to shrimp favorites and Filipino-style seafood boils.

With a casual dining atmosphere, bold flavors, and friendly service, we’re a local favorite for group meals and memorable seafood cravings.
