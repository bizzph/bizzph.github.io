About BOCA

A cozy restaurant in Mandaue Cebu for memorable meals

BOCA is a highly rated Western restaurant in Mandaue City known for premium comfort food, quiet ambiance, cozy interiors, and warm, accommodating service. It is a favorite choice for dates, birthdays, family gatherings, and relaxed dinners with friends.

With private dining rooms, polished interiors, and a menu featuring steaks, pasta, salads, soups, meat dishes, and desserts, BOCA offers an upscale casual experience that feels both intimate and welcoming.
