How this works and its limits

The app reads angular motion from the phone, estimates the smooth camera path, and renders the live video into a cropped canvas with compensating translation and rotation.

It mainly corrects rotational shake. Walking bounce and sideways translation cannot be measured accurately from a gyroscope alone. Browser sensor timing and camera latency also vary by phone.
